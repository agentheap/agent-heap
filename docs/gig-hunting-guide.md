## Outreach Template

### Cold DM (keep it short):
> Hi! I'm a Solidity freelancer specializing in ERC20 tokens and DeFi
> contracts. I saw you mentioned needing a smart contract — happy to help
> with development. Portfolio: https://github.com/yourhandle.
> Quick turnaround, tested code, competitive rates. Interested?

### Gig Proposal (on Upwork etc):
> I've built multiple ERC20 tokens with custom tax mechanisms, ERC721
> NFT collections with reveal mechanics, and DeFi staking/vesting
> contracts. All contracts are OpenZeppelin-based, Hardhat-tested, and
> gas-optimized.
>
> **What I deliver:**
> - Audited-ready Solidity code
> - Unit tests (95%+ coverage)
> - Deployment script
> - Verification on Etherscan/Blockscout
> - Documentation
>
> **Timeline:** 2-5 days depending on complexity
>
> Check my portfolio: [link]
>
> Let me know if you'd like a free quote.
