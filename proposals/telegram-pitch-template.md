## Tips

- **Don't DM first** — comment helpfully in public, then people will come to you
- **Share a snippet** — a Solidity contract snippet gets more attention than "I'm available"
- **Reference competition** — "I see someone's looking for a token deploy in #jobs"
- **Have examples ready** — link to your GitHub; Farcaster casts with links get more reach
